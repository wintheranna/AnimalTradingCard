Cat

There are over 500 million domestic cats and they are one of the most popular pets in the world.

  • Latin Name: Felis catus
  • Average Lifespan: 2-16 years
  • Food: Mice, rats and canned food
  • Fun facts: Cats can jump up to six times their height!

Domestic cats usually weigh around 4-5 kilograms. They have powerful night vision, allowing them to see at light levels six times lower than what a human needs in order to see. Cats also have excellent hearing and a powerful sense of smell. Cats come in long hair, short hair, no hair and a mix of different fur colors and patterns.
